Night Boat
Kobayashi Issa
1763
A night boat
 Sails away
   Illuminated by a wildfire.

1718
In the morning breeze
  Skylarks
    Dance straight up in the sky.

1867
Double cherry blossoms
  Flutter in the wind 
    One petal after another.

Mountain Cherry Blossoms

1892
Mountain cherry blossoms
  Against the snowy peak --
    Silent harmony in heaven.

Tranquil Evening
Yosa Buson
1716
Grasses are misty,
  The waters silent --
    A tranquil evening.

1716
Caught in a sudden shower
  Huddling sparrows 
    Vie to get at grass leaves.

Spring Equinox

1885
On the spring equinox
  Clouds wander about
    The entrance of a mountain temple.

Lotus Leaves
Nakamura Teijo
1900
Withered lotus leaves,
  Some broken, some not,
    Float on the spring water.

Meditation
Kadokawa Haruki
1942
Meditating, a poet
  Squats in the abyss
    Of the ancient cherry blossoms.

Waterfall
Yosa Buson
1784
Young leaves --
  The sound of a waterfall
    Heard from far and near.

Clouds

1763

  Take the shape of a demon,
    Then change into  the Buddha.

The Fly
Kobayashi Issa
1763
The fly
  Wringing its hands and rubbing its feet--
    Don't swat it.

Summer skylarks

Rebirth

1718

  A hilltop pine tree
    Is the image of my rebirth.

1662

  As if coming through the bottom
    Of loneliness.
